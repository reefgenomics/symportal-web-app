SymPortal

a novel analytical framework and platform for coral algal symbiont NGS ITS2 profiling

Notice: From 14.07.2021 new submissions to SymPortal will be embargoed for a period of 1 year.
After this time they will be made public for querying via SymPortal's DataExplorer.
Embargo extensions are available upon request.
Submissions made before 14.07.2021 are unaffected by this change and will remain embargoed indefinitely or until published.
Please see Submit Data for more details.

When submitting:
Please do not remove primers from your reads. These are required by the Symportal QC pipeline.
Please ensure that your read length allows for at least a 30bp overlap: 2x150bp reads are generally insufficient!
Please follow the format guidelines in the data submission sheet carefully.
Please double check that you are submitting the correct dataset (submitting 16S or RNASeq datasets wastes everyones time :))
